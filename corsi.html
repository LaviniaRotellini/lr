Machine Learning

The objectives of the course are to introduce the principles and methods of learning from datasets, with a particular focus on neural networks and their evolutions, which are currently the foundation for building intelligent and adaptive systems across many innovative application domains in computer science and interdisciplinary fields. The course aims to provide the fundamentals of Machine Learning for the purposes of constructing both new adaptive intelligent systems and predictive models for data analysis. In particular, the course focuses on the critical analysis of the characteristics for the implementation and appropriate use of learning algorithms of functions from examples, as well as for modeling and rigorous experimental evaluation.

Text Analytics

The course targets text analytics systems and applications to respond to business problems by discovering and presenting knowledge that is otherwise locked in textual form. The main objectives of the course are: Learning essential techniques, algorithms, and models used in natural language processing. Understanding of the architectures of typical text analytics applications and of libraries for building them. Expertise in design, implementation, and evaluation of applications that exploit analysis, interpretation, and transformation of texts.
